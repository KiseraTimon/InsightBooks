Our Journey

2018

The Seed

InSight Books was born as a neighborhood community book exchange, focused on children's storytelling circles.

2020

Sprouting Online

We launched our first digital portal, allowing remote readers to order rare translated classics directly online.

2022

Physical Reading Spaces

Opened our physical library rooms, creating study cells and stationery shops in three main city centers.

2026

The Digital Chapter

Fully redesigned our digital catalog with real-time searches, sidebar cart drawers, and community hubs.
